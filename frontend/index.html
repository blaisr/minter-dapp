Our Story

Existing in the creative space and supporting creative and community projects with values on creativity, collaboration and community our aim with the Block Peeps metaverse is to provide a realistic PFP / avatar but with fun and exciting traits that exemplify the unlimited and wondrous creative possibilities of digital art and the metaverse. This is the genesis collection for Block Peeps and is only the beginning with other projects in the pipeline. Block Peeps Bros is a collection recognizing and representing strong, loving and inspiring bros. The variety of clothing and fashion styles was to offer more preference, personal taste and individuality into the collection. Cool trendy fashion threads that look great!

Block Peeps is focused on community involvement and shared value and growth with the aim of giving back. We all grow together and it is astonishing what can be achieved through connectedness and collaboration. Block Peeps is special because it will involve collectible music as well as art NFTs and be focused on collaboration with other NFT projects. The Block Peeps Bros genesis collection is significant because it is our first and supports new development and future projects. By supporting this project you are becoming part of our family and supporting art and creative projects aimed at giving back.

We are community focused and always listening to how we can grow together. We understand the value collectibles and projects create for owners and we are committed to project growth and development.

FAQ

  • Why be an Early Adopter and Collector?

    The first 100 wallets that mint will receive 1 FREE Block Peeps Bros NFT. 1 free per wallet for only the first 100 wallets that mint.

  • Where do I mint Block Peeps Bros?

    You can mint Block Peeps Bros via this site and will require a MetaMask wallet and enough MATIC to cover associated costs with minting and gas. If you are eligible to mint then you will be able to click the mint button and follow through the transaction. After a successful minting transaction your NFT(s) will appear inside your wallet.

  • What Is The Mint Price?

    Block Peeps Bros are available to mint for 32 MATIC. You can use your exchange of choice including Transak to get MATIC for the Polygon chain.

  • When can I mint?

    Our public sale is open so you can mint now. Please only mint via official links like this website to ensure authenticity and safe minting.

  • Which marketplace will Block Peeps Bros be listed on?

    After your Block Peeps Bros NFT in minted it will be listed on OpenSea under the Block Peeps Bros Collection.

  • How do I mint?

    Connect your MetaMask wallet on our website and choose the number of Block Peeps Bros you want to mint. Follow the instructions inside your wallet and adjust the gas prices according to your preference to complete minting. After minting your Block Peeps Bros will appear in on your Opensea profile wallet.

  • What is the Supply?

    There are a total of 10K strong, loving and inspiring Block Peeps Bros available. This collection is limited and will not increase in size. We reserve a small amount for giveaways and gifts.

  • What is this collection about?

    This collection is a PFP style NFT project providing a collectible of a realistic avatar but with fun and exciting traits that exemplify the creative possibilities of digital art and the metaverse. The Block Peeps metaverse is growing and this genesis collection is recognizing strong, loving and inspiring bros.

  • What are the benefits of owning a Block Peeps Bros NFT?

    Owning a Block Peeps Bros NFT shows your support for artists and also provides you with a fabulous collectible NFT that can be used as a social media avatar, PFP, used in creative works such as memes, marketing and more. You also benefit from being part of our exclusive community with eligibility for giveaways and utilities.

  • On what block do Block Peeps Bros live?

    Block Peeps Bros live on the Polygon chain. This is a layer 2 Ethereum chain that provides greatly reduced gas fees.

  • What makes this collection special?

    Creativity has no boundaries and with this collection we meshed the best of different creative techniques. We set out to provide realistic avatars but introduce into the collection fun, creative and exciting traits that represent the endless possibilities of the metaverse and digital art. We equally paid attention to trendy, unique and fresh fashion accessories and clothing to further extend the personality, preference and individuality of each Block Peeps Bro!

  • How many can I purchase?

    You can purchase up to 10 NFTs per transaction. This is to make minting fair and available for everyone allowing for more opportunity for art lovers and collectors to join the community.
